Research

Working Paper · PhD Project

Market Structure, Emission Tax, and R&D Incentives: Cournot Oligopoly

A game-theoretic model of optimal emission taxation under imperfect competition. Competition is measured through product substitutability — a fundamental demand parameter — rather than the number of firms. Abatement technology generates spatial spillovers: a firm's investment in cleaner production also reduces emissions from co-located neighbours. Motivated by the policy context of eco-industrial parks in Russia.

MSc Thesis · 2025

Evaluating the 2016 Russian Environmental Policy Reform

An empirical study assessing the causal effect of Russia's 2016 environmental policy reform using panel data methods and quasi-experimental identification strategies.
